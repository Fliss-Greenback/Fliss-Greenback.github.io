Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset

Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people. The idea that it is possible to constantly learn and become more proficient at something the more you practice / solidify the learning is beneficial to everyone - knowing that there is no such thing as stupid, only not interested or can’t be bothered to learn. Also neuroplasticity is the ability for the brain to adapt and rewire itself when it recognises a need to adapt to its environment - that is deeply and fascinatingly useful in current times. Discuss how might engage with the principals of neuroplasticity for your own benefit. I feel like I do already and have done for some time. I really enjoy learning new things but could do a lot better at giving myself more opportunities to solidify the learnings I am gaining through EDA in implementing them within different contexts in a more practical sense (understanding that I definitely learn through handson)

  • Exercise boosts grey matter
  • Making music
  • Playing video games
  • Travel
  • Doodling / make art - practice unfocusing (allows the brain to do other things)
